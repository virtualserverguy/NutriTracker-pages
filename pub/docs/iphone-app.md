# iPhone App Guide

The FuelFlow iPhone app is your nutrition command center. Use it to plan sessions, manage your food library, review history, and track nutrition during activities.

## Interface Overview

The app has four main tabs:

### 1. Dashboard Tab
Your activity hub showing:
- **Active session** (if running) - Current stats and quick logging
- **Start Session** button - Launch a new activity
- **Recent sessions** - Quick access to past activities
- **Today's summary** - Overview of today's nutrition

### 2. Library Tab
Your personal nutrition database:
- **All food items** - Searchable and filterable
- **Categories** - Gels, Bars, Drinks, Food
- **Add items** - Create custom nutrition entries
- **Edit/delete** - Manage your library

### 3. History Tab
Complete activity log:
- **All past sessions** - Sorted by date (newest first)
- **Session cards** - Quick stats for each activity
- **Detailed views** - Tap any session for full breakdown
- **Filter by type** - Show only specific activities

### 4. Settings Tab
App preferences:
- **iCloud sync status** - Check sync health
- **Notifications** - Configure reminders
- **Units** - Metric or Imperial
- **Export data** - Backup your sessions
- **About** - App version and info

## Managing Your Food Library

### Adding Food Items

1. Tap the **Library** tab
2. Tap the **+** button (top right)
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name:** Product name (e.g., "Maurten Gel 100")
   - **Carbs:** Grams of carbohydrates
   - **Sodium:** Milligrams of sodium
   - **Category:** Gel, Bar, Drink, or Food
4. Tap **Save**

**Pro Tips:**
- Use consistent naming (e.g., "Gel - Maurten 100" or "Drink - Tailwind Raspberry")
- Include brand names for easy identification
- Round to nearest 5g/mg for simplicity
- Use the same units you'll see on race day

### Editing Food Items

1. Tap the **Library** tab
2. Find the item you want to edit
3. Tap on the item to open details
4. Tap **Edit** (top right)
5. Make changes
6. Tap **Save**

### Deleting Food Items

**Single item:**
1. Swipe left on the item
2. Tap **Delete**

**Multiple items:**
1. Tap **Edit** (top right)
2. Select items to delete
3. Tap **Delete** button
4. Confirm

**Note:** Deleted items remain in historical session logs.

### Organizing by Category

Use categories to keep your library organized:

- **Gels:** Energy gels, chews, blocks
- **Bars:** Energy bars, waffles
- **Drinks:** Sports drinks, electrolyte mixes
- **Food:** Bananas, sandwiches, real food

During activities, you can filter by category for faster logging.

## Starting a Session

### Planning Your Activity

1. Tap **Start Session** on the Dashboard
2. Select your **activity type:**
   - **Run** - Road runs, trail runs, ultras
   - **Cycle** - Road cycling, mountain biking, gravel
   - **Workout** - Gym, CrossFit, general training
   - **Race** - Race day, time trials, events

3. Set your **nutrition goals** (per hour):
   - **Carbs:** Target grams/hour (typically 30-90g)
   - **Sodium:** Target mg/hour (typically 300-1000mg)
   - **Water:** Target liters/hour (typically 0.4-0.8L)

4. Review and tap **Start**

**Goal Setting Tips:**
- **Training:** Use tested rates from previous sessions
- **Race Day:** Aim for upper end of trained intake
- **Hot Weather:** Increase water and sodium targets
- **Long Events:** Consider lower carb rates for sustainability

### During Your Session

When a session is active, the Dashboard shows:

#### Real-Time Stats
- **Elapsed time** - How long you've been active
- **Current intake** - What you've consumed so far
- **Hourly rates** - Current pace vs. goals
- **Progress bars** - Visual representation of goals

#### Quick Actions
- **Log Food** - Add nutrition intake
- **Log Water** - Record hydration
- **View Timeline** - See all logs chronologically
- **End Session** - Finish the activity

### Logging Food

1. Tap **Log Food** in the active session
2. Browse or search your food library
3. Tap an item to log it
4. The log is added with current timestamp

**Instant sync:** Your log appears immediately on your Apple Watch (if connected).

**Batch logging:** Forgot to log something? Add items after the fact with automatic timestamps.

### Logging Water

1. Tap **Log Water** in the active session
2. Select amount:
   - **Quick amounts:** 100ml, 250ml, 500ml
   - **Custom:** Enter any amount
3. Confirm to log

**Hydration tracking:** Water logs are included in your session timeline and hourly rate calculations.

### Editing Logs

Made a mistake? Edit or delete logs during your session:

1. Tap **View Timeline**
2. Find the log to modify
3. **Swipe left** for options:
   - **Edit** - Change quantity or time
   - **Delete** - Remove the log
4. Confirm changes

### Live Activities (Lock Screen)

When you start a session, FuelFlow creates a Live Activity that appears on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

**Lock Screen Widget:**
- Current carb, sodium, water intake
- Hourly rates vs. goals
- Elapsed time
- Last logged item

**Dynamic Island (iPhone 14 Pro and newer):**
- **Compact:** Shows elapsed time
- **Expanded:** Full stats with progress bars
- **Tap:** Opens FuelFlow app

**Why it's useful:**
- Quick glances during activities
- No need to unlock phone or open app
- Always-visible progress tracking

## Ending Your Session

1. Tap **End Session**
2. Review the summary:
   - **Duration** - Total active time
   - **Totals** - All carbs, sodium, water consumed
   - **Hourly averages** - Your actual intake rates
   - **Timeline** - Every log with timestamps
3. Tap **Done** to save

The session is automatically saved to History and synced via iCloud.

## Viewing Session History

### Browsing Past Sessions

1. Tap the **History** tab
2. Scroll through your sessions (newest first)
3. Each card shows:
   - Activity type icon
   - Date and duration
   - Total carbs, sodium, water
   - Session status

### Viewing Session Details

Tap any session card to see:

**Summary Stats:**
- Start/end times
- Duration
- Total intake (carbs, sodium, water)
- Goals and actual rates
- Activity type

**Timeline:**
- Every food and water log
- Timestamps
- Quantities
- Chronological order

**Analysis:**
- Hourly intake rates
- Goal achievement percentages
- Nutrition timing distribution

### Filtering Sessions

At the top of History:
- **All** - Show everything
- **Run** - Only running activities
- **Cycle** - Only cycling activities
- **Workout** - Only workouts
- **Race** - Only races

### Searching Sessions

Use the search bar to find sessions by:
- Date
- Activity type
- Duration
- Specific foods consumed

## Settings & Customization

### iCloud Sync

**Status indicator:** Shows sync health
- **Green checkmark:** All data synced
- **Syncing:** Upload/download in progress
- **Error:** Sync issue (check internet/iCloud)

**Manual sync:** Tap to force sync if needed

### Notifications

Configure reminders:
- **During activities:** Remind to log every X minutes
- **Pre-session:** Reminder to prepare nutrition
- **Post-session:** Prompt to review and save

### Units

Choose your preferred display:
- **Carbs:** Grams (g)
- **Sodium:** Milligrams (mg)
- **Water:** Liters (L) or Fluid Ounces (fl oz)
- **Distance:** Kilometers (km) or Miles (mi)

### Data Export

Export your sessions for analysis:
1. Tap **Export Data** in Settings
2. Choose format:
   - **CSV** - Spreadsheet compatible
   - **JSON** - Raw data format
3. Choose date range
4. Share via email, files, or other apps

**Use cases:**
- Backup your data
- Share with coaches
- Analyze in spreadsheets
- Track long-term trends

## Tips & Best Practices

### Pre-Activity Planning
- Add nutrition to your library the night before
- Set realistic goals based on past sessions
- Check iCloud sync status
- Enable notifications if desired

### During Activities
- Use Apple Watch for fastest logging
- Check Lock Screen widget for quick stats
- Don't worry about exact timestamps - the app handles it
- Focus on your activity, log when convenient

### Post-Activity Review
- Review session summary while fresh
- Note what worked well
- Adjust goals for next time
- Export data if analyzing with coach

### Library Management
- Regular cleanup of unused items
- Consistent naming conventions
- Update items as products change
- Add new items as you discover them

### Sync Strategy
- Keep Bluetooth enabled for instant Watch sync
- Ensure iCloud is enabled for backup
- Check sync status before important events
- Both sync methods work together seamlessly

## Troubleshooting

### "Session not starting"
- Check that you've set all required goals
- Ensure you're not already in an active session
- Restart the app if issue persists

### "Food items not showing in library"
- Check iCloud sync status
- Pull down to refresh the library
- Check internet connection
- Wait a moment for sync to complete

### "Live Activity not appearing"
- Check that Live Activities are enabled in iOS Settings
- Go to Settings > FuelFlow > Live Activities
- Ensure notifications are allowed
- Restart session if already active

### "Session not syncing to Watch"
- See [Sync & Data Guide](/docs/sync-and-data/) for detailed troubleshooting
- Check Bluetooth connectivity
- Open app on both devices
- iCloud will sync as backup if Watch isn't reachable

- **⌘W:** Log water

- **⌘[1-4]:** Switch tabs
