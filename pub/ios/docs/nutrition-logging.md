# Nutrition Logging & Food Library Guide

Master your in-activity fueling with FuelFlow's food library and logging system.

---

## Overview

FuelFlow helps you track three critical nutrition metrics during endurance activities:
- **Carbohydrates** - Energy for sustained effort
- **Sodium** - Electrolyte balance and hydration
- **Water** - Hydration and thermoregulation

Build your personal food library once, then log with a single tap during activities.

---

## The Food Library

### What is the Food Library?

Your food library is your personal database of:
- Energy gels and chews
- Sports drinks and mixes
- Bars and solid foods
- Water bottles and hydration
- Custom recipes

**Benefits**:
- One-tap logging during activities
- Consistent nutrition tracking
- Accurate macros for analysis
- Syncs across all your devices (iCloud)

### Accessing the Library

**iPhone**:
- Tap **Library** tab (book icon)
- Or: During activity ‚Üí Log Food ‚Üí **Browse Library**

**Apple Watch**:
- Not available on watch (use iPhone to manage)
- During activity: Quick list shows favorites only

## Adding Food Items

### Quick Add (iPhone)

1. Library tab ‚Üí Tap **+** button
2. Enter food details:
   - **Name**: "Maurten Gel 100" or "SiS Beta Fuel"
   - **Category**: Gel, Bar, Drink, Food, Other
   - **Carbs**: 25g (example)
   - **Sodium**: 100mg (example)
   - **Volume**: Leave blank for solid food
3. Tap **Save**

**Tip**: Use the exact product name for easy identification later.

### Adding with Volume (Drinks)

For drinks and mixes:
1. Name: "Maurten 320 Mix"
2. Category: **Drink**
3. Carbs: 80g
4. Sodium: 800mg
5. **Volume**: 500ml (per bottle)
6. Save

Now when you log this drink, FuelFlow tracks both nutrition AND hydration!

### Product Categories

**Gel** - Energy gels, shots (Maurten, SiS, GU, Huma, etc.)
**Bar** - Energy bars, waffles (Clif, PROBAR, Honey Stinger, etc.)
**Drink** - Sports drinks, mixes (Maurten, Tailwind, Skratch, etc.)
**Food** - Real food (bananas, PB&J, rice balls, etc.)
**Other** - Anything else (salt caps, chews, etc.)

## Managing Your Library

### Editing Food Items

1. Library tab ‚Üí Tap food item
2. Edit any field (name, carbs, sodium, volume)
3. Tap **Save**

**Note**: Changes apply to future logs only, not past activities.

### Deleting Food Items

1. Library tab ‚Üí Swipe left on item
2. Tap **Delete**
3. Confirm

**Warning**: Deleting a food doesn't remove it from past activity logs.

### Favorites

**Why Use Favorites?**
- Appear at top of library
- Show in quick-log list during activities
- Available on Apple Watch
- Faster access to go-to products

**Adding to Favorites**:
- Tap star icon (‚≠ê) next to food item
- Or: Tap food ‚Üí **Add to Favorites**

**Removing from Favorites**:
- Tap filled star (‚òÖ) to unfavorite

**Best Practice**: Favorite 5-10 products you use most often.

## Logging Nutrition During Activities

### Quick Log (iPhone)

**During Active Session**:
1. Tap **Log Food** button
2. Choose from:
   - **Favorites** (top of list)
   - **Recent** (last used items)
   - **All Library** (scroll to find)
3. Tap item ‚Üí Logged!

**Custom Amount**:
- Long-press item ‚Üí **Custom Amount**
- Adjust quantity (e.g., "1.5 gels" or "Half bar")

### Quick Log (Apple Watch)

**During Active Session**:
1. Raise wrist
2. Scroll to **Favorites list**
3. Tap item ‚Üí Logged!

**Syncs to iPhone in <1 second** via Bluetooth.

### Logging Water

**Water Bottles** (pre-configured):
1. Tap **Log Water**
2. Choose:
   - Water Bottle (500ml)
   - Water Cup (250ml)
   - Custom amount

**From Drinks**:
- If you log a drink with volume (e.g., Maurten 500ml bottle)
- FuelFlow automatically adds 500ml to water intake!

**Custom Water Amount**:
1. Log Water ‚Üí **Custom**
2. Enter ml/oz
3. Confirm

## Nutrition Tracking Strategies

### During Short Activities (<2 hours)

**Simple Approach**:
- Pre-load 2-3 favorites
- Log as you consume
- Don't overthink it

**Example Setup**:
- Favorite: "GU Energy Gel" (carbs only)
- Favorite: "Nuun Tablet" (sodium + water)
- Log when you take them

### During Long Activities (2-6 hours)

**Structured Approach**:
- Set hourly goals (60g carbs, 500mg sodium, 600ml water)
- Log every gel, sip, bite
- Check progress every 30-60 min

**Example Routine**:
- :00 - Start activity
- :30 - Gel + Water (log both)
- 1:00 - Check status (are you on track?)
- 1:30 - Gel + Electrolyte drink
- Repeat...

### During Ultra Events (6+ hours)

**Flexible Approach**:
- Front-load carbs in first 2 hours
- Mix gels + real food (avoid gut issues)
- Track sodium closely (heavy sweating)

**Pro Tips**:
- Use favorites for race fuel
- Add custom "Aid Station" item (mixed nutrition)
- Log immediately (memory fades when fatigued!)

## Logging Custom/Unknown Foods

### Quick Custom Log

**If you don't have time to add to library**:
1. Log Food ‚Üí **Quick Custom**
2. Enter:
   - Carbs: 30g
   - Sodium: 200mg
   - (Optional) Notes: "Mystery gel at aid station"
3. Log

**Saved temporarily** - won't appear in library.

### Adding Unknown Food to Library

**At aid stations with unfamiliar products**:
1. Take photo of nutrition label (later)
2. During activity: Log as "Quick Custom" with estimate
3. After activity: Add accurate info to library

## Analyzing Your Nutrition

### During Activity

**Check Your Progress**:
- iPhone: Main screen shows live carbs/sodium/water
- Watch: Raise wrist for quick glance
- Live Activity: Lock screen widgets

**Color Codes**:
- üü¢ Green: On target (80-120% of goal)
- üü° Yellow: Slightly off (60-80% or 120-150%)
- üî¥ Red: Action needed (<60% or >150%)

**What to Do**:
- Green ‚Üí Keep it up!
- Yellow ‚Üí Adjust next intake
- Red (under) ‚Üí Extra gel/water soon
- Red (over) ‚Üí Slow down intake

### After Activity

**Review What Worked**:
1. Activity Detail ‚Üí Nutrition tab
2. See timeline of all logs:
   - "30 min: Maurten Gel (25g carbs)"
   - "1:00: Water Bottle (500ml)"
3. Total consumed vs. goals

**On Route Map**:
- Nutrition markers show where you logged
- Correlate with terrain/effort
- Example: "I always bonk at mile 18 ‚Üí need gel at mile 16"

## Troubleshooting

### Food Library Not Syncing

**Check**:
- iCloud signed in (Settings ‚Üí Apple ID)
- Internet connection available
- FuelFlow has iCloud permission

**Force Sync**:
- Settings ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Tap **Sync Now**

### Duplicate Food Items

**How it Happens**:
- Added same product twice
- Synced from multiple devices

**To Fix**:
- Keep the one with most accurate info
- Delete duplicates (swipe left)

### Favorites Not Appearing on Watch

**Check**:
- Item starred as favorite (‚≠ê)
- Watch paired and FuelFlow installed
- Force sync: Open FuelFlow on watch

**Still not showing?**
- Un-favorite and re-favorite on iPhone
- Restart watch

### Lost Food Library After Reinstall

**If you deleted and reinstalled FuelFlow**:
- Food library should restore from iCloud (wait 5-10 min)
- If not: Contact support@fuelflow.run

## Tips & Best Practices

### Building Your Library

1. **Start small** - Add 5-10 products you actually use
2. **Use brand names** - "SiS Beta Fuel" not "Gel 1"
3. **Be accurate** - Check nutrition labels for carbs/sodium
4. **Favorite wisely** - Only star what you use every activity
5. **Review quarterly** - Remove products you stopped using

### Logging Strategy

1. **Log immediately** - Don't wait until end of run
2. **Use voice** - Shortcuts for hands-free logging
3. **Check progress** - Glance every 30-60 min
4. **Trust your body** - Goals are guides, not rules
5. **Adjust based on conditions** - Hot day = more sodium/water

### Race Day Prep

1. **Create race-specific favorites**:
   - "Race Day Gel" (your proven fuel)
   - "Aid Station Cola" (if you'll drink it)
   - "Water Cup" (250ml from aid stations)
2. **Practice logging** on long training runs
3. **Test new products** in training, never on race day
4. **Pre-load library** before race (avoid fumbling mid-race)
