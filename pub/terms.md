## 2. Description of Service

FuelFlow is a nutrition tracking application designed to help athletes monitor and optimize their fueling strategies during endurance activities. The App provides:

- **Nutrition Logging**: Track carbohydrate, sodium, and water intake
- **Activity Tracking**: Monitor consumption during runs and other endurance activities
- **Food Library**: Create and manage a personal database of nutrition items
- **Data Synchronization**: Sync data across your devices via iCloud (Apple) or Google Cloud (Android)

FuelFlow is a tool to assist with nutrition tracking. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical, nutritional, or health advice.
